Cocktails

Ingredients & Instructions
Boulevardier ▼

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Bourbon or Rye Whiskey
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
  • Orange twist (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add bourbon/rye, Campari, and sweet vermouth to a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass with ice or a coupe glass.
  4. Garnish with an orange twist.
Cosmopolitan ▼

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Cranberry juice
  • 0.5 oz Cointreau or triple sec
  • 0.5 oz Fresh lime juice
  • Lime wheel or twist (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add vodka, cranberry juice, Cointreau, and lime juice to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with a lime wheel or twist.
Cuba Libre ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz White rum
  • 0.5 oz Fresh lime juice
  • Cola
  • Lime wedge (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Add rum and lime juice.
  3. Top with cola and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge.
Daiquiri ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz White rum
  • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 0.75 oz Simple syrup
  • Lime wheel (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add rum, lime juice, and simple syrup to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a chilled coupe glass.
  3. Garnish with a lime wheel.
Highball ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Whiskey (Japanese, Scotch, or other)
  • Chilled soda water
  • Lemon twist (for garnish, optional)

Instructions

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Add whiskey.
  3. Top with chilled soda water and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a lemon twist if desired.
Long Island Iced Tea ▼

Ingredients

  • 0.5 oz Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Gin
  • 0.5 oz White rum
  • 0.5 oz Tequila
  • 0.5 oz Triple sec
  • 0.75 oz Lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz Simple syrup
  • Cola
  • Lemon wedge (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add all spirits, lemon juice, and simple syrup to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake briefly and strain into a tall glass filled with ice.
  3. Top with cola and garnish with a lemon wedge.
Manhattan ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Rye or Bourbon
  • 1 oz Sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Cherry (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add whiskey, vermouth, and bitters to a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
  4. Garnish with a cherry.
Margarita ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz Lime juice
  • 0.75 oz Cointreau or triple sec
  • Salt (for rim, optional)
  • Lime wheel (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Rim a glass with salt if desired.
  2. Add tequila, lime juice, and Cointreau to a shaker with ice.
  3. Shake well and strain into the glass over fresh ice.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel.
Martini ▼

Ingredients

  • 2.5 oz Gin or Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Dry vermouth
  • Lemon twist or olive (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add gin or vodka and vermouth to a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir (or shake, if preferred) until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  4. Garnish with a lemon twist or olive.

You can make a martini with either gin or vodka.

Mojito ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz White rum
  • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 6-8 Mint leaves
  • Soda water
  • Mint sprig & lime wedge (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Muddle mint leaves and sugar with lime juice in a glass.
  2. Fill glass with ice, add rum.
  3. Top with soda water and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wedge.
Moscow Mule ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Lime juice
  • Ginger beer
  • Lime wedge (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Fill a copper mug or glass with ice.
  2. Add vodka and lime juice.
  3. Top with ginger beer and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge.
Negroni ▼

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Campari

  • Orange peel (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth to a mixing glass with ice.

Old Fashioned ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Bourbon or Rye whiskey
  • 1 sugar cube
  • 2-3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Muddle sugar and bitters in a rocks glass.
  2. Add whiskey and ice, stir until chilled.
  3. Garnish with an orange peel.
Piña Colada ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz White rum
  • 1 oz Coconut cream
  • 1 oz Heavy cream (optional)
  • 6 oz Fresh pineapple juice
  • Pineapple wedge & cherry (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender with ice.
  2. Blend until smooth and pour into a chilled glass.
  3. Garnish with pineapple wedge and cherry.
Tokyo Tea ▼

Ingredients

  • 0.5 oz Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Gin
  • 0.5 oz White rum
  • 0.5 oz Tequila
  • 0.5 oz Midori (melon liqueur)
  • 0.5 oz Triple sec
  • 1 oz Sweet & sour mix
  • Lemon-lime soda
  • Lemon wedge (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add all spirits and sweet & sour mix to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a tall glass filled with ice.
  3. Top with lemon-lime soda and garnish with a lemon wedge.
Whiskey Sour ▼

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Bourbon or Rye whiskey
  • 0.75 oz Fresh lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz Simple syrup
  • Egg white (optional)
  • Cherry & orange slice (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white (if using) to a shaker without ice. Dry shake well.
  2. Add ice and shake again until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass with ice.
  4. Garnish with a cherry and orange slice.

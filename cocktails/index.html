  • Negroni
Negroni ▼

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
  • Orange peel (for garnish)

Method

  1. Add gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth to a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice.
  4. Garnish with an orange peel.
